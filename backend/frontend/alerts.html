🚨 Active Scam Alerts

Stay aware of the most common scams currently affecting users.

📌 What is happening?

  • Fake OTP calls claiming to be from banks
  • Fraudulent job offers asking for registration fees
  • Phishing emails asking login credentials

⚠️ Why this is dangerous

These scams trick users into sharing sensitive information like passwords, OTPs, or money. Once compromised, accounts and finances can be misused.

📱 Example

"Your bank account will be blocked. Click this link and verify your details immediately."

🛡️ How to stay safe

  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Do not click unknown links
  • Verify messages through official sources
