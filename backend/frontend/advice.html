✨ Safety Advice

Follow these essential tips to protect yourself from scams.

🛡️ Best Practices

  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Avoid clicking unknown links
  • Verify identity before making payments

⚠️ Warning Signs

  • Urgent requests for money
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers
  • Unknown senders asking personal info

📌 Golden Rule

If something feels urgent or suspicious, pause and verify.
